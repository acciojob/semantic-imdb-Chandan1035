3 Idiots

Actors Quotes
"3 Idiots" is a popular Indian film released in 2009. It is a comedy-drama directed by Rajkumar Hirani and stars Aamir Khan, Kareena Kapoor, and R. Madhavan. The film is based on the novel "Five Point Someone" by Chetan Bhagat, and follows the lives of three engineering students who challenge the status quo and question the value of formal education.

Actors

  • Aamir Khan as Ranchoddas "Rancho" Shamaldas Chanchad
  • Kareena Kapoor as Pia Sahastrabudhhe
  • R. Madhavan as Farhan Qureshi
  • Sharman Joshi as Raju Rastogi
